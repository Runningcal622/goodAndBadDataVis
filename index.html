A good Example of Data Visualization

  1. Functionality
    This infographic is functional because it shows current data through the pie charts and then shows the trends through the line graphs to the right. It clearly demonstates the fact that the most people believe that the best is ahead of us.
  2. Beautiful
    This infographic is generally pleasing because of the light blue and yellow they used to differentiate the different responses. However, it could be improved because the light blue is so often used. Maybe using different colors would make the globe stand out more from the rest of the background.
  3. Insightful
    This infographic is insightful because of the way it uses past data to show the trend among patents. The United States has been the leader in recent past and while that remains true in this data; it is interesting to see Japan and China growing rapidly while the US declines. It is also helpful that the colors used for the lines in the graph match up with the colors used for the opinions. If a country is showing a negative correlation then it is colored with a "behind" response color.
  4. Truthful
    This infographic is truthful because it does not seek to decieve the viewer of information important to the graphic. All graphs have only one y-axis. This info graphic is used to display data, not force you to an incorrect conclusion.
  5. Enlightening
    This infographic is important because it seeks to inform on the topic of wether the collective optimism of progress will continue and lead to more improvements. The idea of self fufilling prophecy is vital becasue if people think that progress is in the past then it could occur soley because people think that it can't.

  2. Beautiful

  3. Insightful
    This infographic is not insightful because most of the stuff it talks about are self evident. In a war we would need to restore the military; yes but that doesnt tell me much. It seems mostly at a superficial level.
  4. Truthful
    This infographic is absent many facts and data that would make it appealing. Most of the steps are vague and not well defined. In the middle it just says "3 trillion dollars", but why 3?
  5. Enlightening
